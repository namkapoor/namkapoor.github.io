I am a final year BS-MS Physics student at the Indian Institute of Technology (IIT) Roorkee, graduating in May 2026. Currently ranked first in my department with a CGPA of 9.58/10, I am passionate about nanophotonics and quantum technologies.

My research interests lie at the intersection of fundamental physics and practical applications, with a focus on experimental and computational work in nanophotonics. I have worked on projects ranging from chromatic metalenses to topological photonic crystals and integrated photon pair sources.

I will begin my Master's thesis in July 2025 and am currently preparing for PhD applications for Fall 2026. I am particularly interested in nanophotonic applications including AR/VR, LIDAR, and optical interconnects.
